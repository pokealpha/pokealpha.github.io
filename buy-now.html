Let us help you!

Before you pull the trigger...

Please do be sure to take a look at our frequently asked questions to better understand how we work.

Reach out to our staff

As each account is meticulously handled, we ask that you reach out to our staff either via the contact form, or our live chat, to help us better understand your needs. Our management staff will go over your situation (including any eligibility for discounts) with you, and hand on the information to a talented Pokémon trainer in your region.

How does payment work?

Here at PokéAlpha, we try to make the process as fair as possible for everyone. We will need you to pay a (small) deposit so we can send a trainer on their way. This fee will go towards the total cost of training. Although we pay our trainers per hour, we bill a flat amount to you. Every few hours of progress, we ask that you pay a portion of the total cost. Or if you'd prefer a one time fee, we can do that also.
