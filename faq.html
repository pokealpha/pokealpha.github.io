Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pay to have my account trained?

Absolutely, we're working hard to offer a comprehensive set of services to best help accomplish your goals.

Part of that mission is to provide both leveling accounts from scratch, and leveling your existing account for you. When contacting us, be sure to mention you wish to level an existing account, and provide the credentials. Depending on the level of your existing account, we may be able to offer a discount for the work you've already invested into your own journey as a Pokémon master!

Can I trust your trainers?

PokéAlpha trainers are people who have travelled far and wide to be the very best. They have deep knowledge about everything there is to know about being a Pokémon master; the best farming locations, where to find the rarest Pokémon, IV calculations, the strongest movesets for gym battles, and much much more. Most important of all, every trainer is required to have an impressive track record and regular updates proving their journey.

Our trainers are hired using a rigorous set of requirements to ensure we can provide you the best experience no one else is able to. The people we work with are all people who have gained our trust over the period of a couple accounts. New trainers working for us are not permitted to train on a player's existing account; only new accounts. Any breach of this trust results in a permanent ban from our services and freezing of payment.

That being said, just like hiring anybody, there is some risk involved, but we do take your account information very seriously, and will work closely with you in the case a trusted trainer goes rogue.

Will you beat gyms for me?

We primarily provide services to help you level up, and capture strong pokemon; having our trainers tackle the miserable grind to help you get where you want to be. That being said, if our trainer is located in your region, we are able to help you beat gyms and gain Pokécoins services for you at additional cost. Reach out to our friendly management team for more information. Do note if you'd like to tackle them yourself, our trainers work hard you get you strong Pokémon that you can power up and take into battle!

I live in country X, can you help me?

We currently have trainers located in: Canada, America, United Kingdom, Europe, Australia (N.S.W/Queensland only), and most of Asia. Unfortunately, we do not yet have trainers from Africa or India, but are always looking for qualified people. If you are from those regions, please do apply!

I have a disability, can I still use your service?

Absolutely! In fact, depending on your situation, we may be able to offer you a discount (on us)!

Due to popular demand, we're now offering discounts to players with mobility disabilities to help level the playing field. Mention your disability along with proof of disability documentation, and we'll work with you to accomplish your dreams!

I'm the best Pokémon trainer, can I join your team?

Take a look at our careers page for an opening that suits you, then contact us and we'd be happy to go over your qualifications.
