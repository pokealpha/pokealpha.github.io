Many of us have dreamed of becoming the very best like no one ever has. Achieving those dreams however, has been another issue. Now that the possibility is real again, we've assembled a great team to help make your dreams a reality, too.

Our arsenal of the best Pokémon trainers from all over the world can take your mind off the stress and enable you to succeed.

What makes us awesome?

PokéAlpha trainers are people who have travelled far and wide to be the very best. They have deep knowledge about everything there is to know about being a Pokémon master; the best farming locations, where to find the rarest Pokémon, IV calculations, the strongest movesets for gym battles, and much much more. Most important of all, every trainer is required to have an impressive track record and regular updates proving their journey.

Disabilities?

Do you have disabilities that prevent you from effectively becoming a true Pokémon master? Is your disability mobility related? We hear you. Due to popular demand, we're now offering discounts to players with mobility disabilities to help level the playing field. Mention your disability along with proof of disability documentation, and we'll work with you to accomplish your dreams!
